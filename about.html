About

Professor and
Corporate Relations Member

Prof. Dr. Dhanashree Tharkude is PhD in Human Resource Management having more than 20 years of experience in an academic and industry.

At MIT World Peace University Faculty of Management (PG), currently she is working as Assistant Professor for HR Domain, Class In-charge CSR Year I and Corporate Relations member.

Along with teaching and corporate relations activity, her current role includes closely working with Program Heads, Academic Head and Head of School in the area of course design and student development. She is actively involved in Academic Programming & Planning, Policies & Procedures Development, and Designing Syllabus Framework.

She has a passion to work at various levels helping institute to reach at desired heights. To achieve this passion her few of the successful assignments at MITWPU includes establishing active Industry Advisory Board having more than 50 National & International industry stalwarts, tie up with more than 15 national, multinational industries etc.

Her experience in Academic, Research, Training and Consultancy contributes more to bring the application oriented approach to study and resolve the real life situation. She is motivated to work more in the areas of business models, processes, behavioural studies etc. She prefers to choose unique industry based research problems for research work.

Recently she has filed a Patent in the area of Human Resource Management.

She has published more than 10 Research Papers and 2 Case Studies in renowned National & International journals. She has successfully completed research projects with Cummins India Ltd. And Vinsys IT Services.

She is warded as Best Teacher for the Best Services to the Education in 2016 by Lions Club of Pune in Oct 2016.
